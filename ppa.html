Understanding Power Purchase Agreements (Second Edition)

An open-access guide for governments and decision-makers on structuring and negotiating power purchase agreements in emerging markets.

Overview

This second edition provides updated insights and case studies on PPA negotiations. It includes new guidance on commercial and industrial PPAs, and cross-border agreements, contributed by a global group of pro bono experts.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive coverage of PPA structures and risk allocation
  • Real-world case studies
  • Emerging trends in renewables and regional power pools
  • Templates and checklists
